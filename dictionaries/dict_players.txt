Players.csv
- 'Index': unique value incremented from 0
- 'Player': Name of the catalogued player (first and last name)
- 'Height': Height (cm) of the player 
- 'Weight': Weight (kg) of the player
- 'College': College attended by player
- 'Born': Birth year of the player
- 'Birth_city': City the player was born
- 'Birth_state': State the player was born
